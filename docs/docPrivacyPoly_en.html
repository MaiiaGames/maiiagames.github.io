Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 17, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Ball Sort Challenge, developed by Luis Carlos Lima, Independent Developer, handles information in the context of the game. Our goal is to be transparent.

1. Collection and Use of Data for Personalized Ads

The Ball Sort Challenge uses AdMob, an advertising platform, to display ads that help keep the game free. With your consent, AdMob may collect information such as approximate location data to provide personalized and more relevant ads for you. This data is used solely to improve your ad experience by showing products or services you might be interested in. You can change your consent preferences at any time in the game settings. If you do not agree, we will only show non-personalized ads that do not use your personal data. For more information on how AdMob handles your data, please see AdMob’s Privacy Policy at https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

If you have questions about our policy, please contact us at maiiagames@proton.me.

2. No Data Sharing

Since we do not collect any data, we do not share information with third parties, whether service providers, affiliates, business partners, or any other entity. There is no data to share, store, or process.

3. In-Game Purchases

Ball Sort Challenge includes in-app purchases. These transactions are managed solely by the app stores (such as Google Play or App Store). We do not have access to any information related to these transactions, such as payment details or user identification.

4. Children's Privacy

Ball Sort Challenge is suitable for all ages, including children. Since we do not collect any data, there is no risk of collecting information from minors. We do not request or store any information from any user, regardless of age.

5. Links to Other Websites

The game does not contain links to external websites or third-party services, except for rating on the Google Play Store. If you access our Privacy Policy or Terms of Use through a WebView within the game, these documents are local files and do not involve data collection.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if necessary, for example, to reflect changes in the game or legal requirements. The latest version will be available in the game. Since we do not collect contact information, changes will be communicated by updating this page in the application.

7. Legal Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with the Brazilian General Data Protection Law (LGPD) and other applicable laws. Since we do not collect data, no additional data protection measures are required.
